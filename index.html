Fun Facts

  • Texas is larger than any country in Europe
  • Texas is the live-music capital of the world
  • Texas' name is based on a Native American word meaning "friend"

Flavors of Texas

Texas is known for its food and many flavor profiles. When it comes to food, Texas knowns what's up!

The offical dish of Texas is chili, and it has been since 1977. Smoked brisket, ribs, pecan pie, and peach cobbler are the key foods of Texan. And don't forget to top it all off with Blue Bell ice cream.
